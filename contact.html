Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

+

We primarily serve Cork and surrounding areas, but we also take on projects throughout Ireland. Contact us to discuss your location and project needs.

How long does a typical project take?

+

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A residential renovation might take 2-4 months, while a new build could take 6-12 months. We provide detailed timelines during the planning phase.

Do you provide free consultations?

+

Yes, we offer free initial consultations to discuss your project, assess feasibility, and provide preliminary advice. Contact us to schedule a consultation.

What makes your construction eco-friendly?

+

We use sustainable materials, energy-efficient designs, renewable energy systems, and environmentally responsible construction practices. We also focus on reducing waste and minimizing environmental impact throughout the building process.
