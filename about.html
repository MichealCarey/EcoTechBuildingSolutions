About EcoTech Building Solutions

Leading sustainable construction in Ireland

Our Story

EcoTech Building Solutions Ltd has been at the forefront of sustainable construction in Ireland for over 15 years. Founded with a vision to revolutionize the building industry through eco-friendly practices and innovative solutions, we've grown from a small local contractor to a trusted name in sustainable construction.

Based in Glanmire, Co. Cork, we serve clients across Ireland, delivering exceptional building projects that combine environmental responsibility with outstanding craftsmanship. Our commitment to sustainability isn't just a trend—it's the foundation of everything we do.
